Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our lighting services

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for weddings and large events. For smaller events, 2-4 weeks notice is usually sufficient.

Do you provide setup and teardown?

Yes, all our packages include professional setup and teardown. Our team handles everything from installation to cleanup.

What if it rains on my outdoor event?

We use weather-resistant equipment and have backup plans for outdoor events. We also offer tent lighting solutions for added protection.

Can you work with my venue's restrictions?

Absolutely! We work closely with venues to ensure all installations meet their requirements and safety standards.

Do you offer emergency support?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency support for all our events. Our team is always available to address any issues immediately.
